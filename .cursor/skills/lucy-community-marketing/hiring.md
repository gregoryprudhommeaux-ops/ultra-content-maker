# LA MESA — Hiring & Role Design (Lucy · ARCHITECT)

## Role title

**Community & Partnerships Lead**  
Aliases: Community & Business Development Lead · Table Curator & Partnerships  
Never: junior Community Manager

## Mission (1 line)

Be the human bridge between LA MESA’s exclusive identity and Guadalajara/Mexico decision-makers — attract the right profiles, compose relevant tables, close seats & partnerships, embody the culture on the ground and online.

## Must-have

- 8–12 years relevant experience; age maturity ~30+
- Real immersion France **and** Mexico (years lived/worked — not passport-only)
- ≥2 of: B2B community · BD/partnerships · premium events/business clubs · institutional/econ diplomacy · expat/chamber networks
- FR C2 · ES mexicano C1–C2 · EN C1 business
- Credible with young founders **and** CEOs/investors
- Reads status/rangos; respects cultural boundaries; discreet
- Has closed (seats, membership, sponsorship, mandate)
- Mix: warm relationship methods + 2026 LinkedIn/WhatsApp norms

## Nice-to-have

- Third international terrain
- Existing GDL/Jalisco/CDMX density
- CCIFM / French Tech / Business France alumni
- Occasional FO/banking/diplomacy altitude (capability, not daily job)

## Anti-profiles

Logistics-only planner · junior scheduler · aggressive hunter · “knows people” without depth · Spanish without Mexican codes · content-only · indiscreet stylish

## Interview prompts (sample)

- Walk me through a group you curated and how you decided who was **out**.
- Tell me about closing a premium offer without sounding salesy — MX context preferred.
- Role-play: invite a busy industrial CEO in Jalisco by WhatsApp (ES).
- Role-play: explain waitlist to a confused French member (FR).
- Scenario: two direct competitors both want the same table — what do you do?
- What’s a social mistake French expats make in GDL business? Mexican founders with French investors?

**Red flags:** talks only content metrics; can’t decline anyone; mocks Mexican “slow” trust; no proof of close; name-drops without substance; wants to “go viral”.

## Comp logic (guidance)

Phase 1 (2–3 mo): **senior international fee** (not local junior CM rate) + variable on qualified signups / paid seats / signed partners.  
Phase 2: formalize; discuss revenue share / equity if long-term fit.  
Ticket revenue is partial; **partnerships/private editions** are major upside — align variable accordingly.
